Respect for Intellectual Property

Tarifter respects the intellectual property rights of others and complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). We do not host or store any copyrighted content on our servers. Our Service acts as a technical tool that processes publicly available URLs provided by users.

How Tarifter Works

Tarifter does not host, upload, or permanently store any video or audio files. When a user requests a download:

  • The user provides a public URL from a supported platform
  • Our server temporarily processes the request
  • The processed file is delivered to the user's device
  • Temporary files are automatically deleted within 1 hour

We do not index, catalog, or make any content searchable or discoverable through our platform.

Filing a DMCA Takedown Notice

If you believe that your copyrighted work has been infringed through the use of our Service, please send a DMCA takedown notice to our designated agent with the following information:

  1. A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized representative.
  2. Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed.
  3. Identification of the material that is claimed to be infringing, including the specific URL(s).
  4. Your contact information (name, address, phone number, and email).
  5. A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner.
  6. A statement, made under penalty of perjury, that the information in the notice is accurate and that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on their behalf.
