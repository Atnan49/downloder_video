What is PPh 21 TER 2026?

The Average Effective Rate (TER) is the newest calculation scheme for Income Tax (PPh 21) deductions for permanent employees as regulated by PP No. 58 of 2023. Applied by the Ministry of Finance to simplify tax calculation to be more transparent. You no longer need to calculate position costs and pension contributions on a monthly basis, simply multiply your Gross Salary by the TER percentage according to your PTKP status.

Differences Between TER Categories A, B, and C

To simplify matters for taxpayers, the government divides the Non-Taxable Income (PTKP) status into three main categories:

Category A

  • Tidak Kawin tanpa tanggungan (TK/0)
  • Tidak Kawin dgn 1 tanggungan (TK/1)
  • Kawin tanpa tanggungan (K/0)

Category B

  • Tidak Kawin dgn 2 tanggungan (TK/2)
  • Tidak Kawin dgn 3 tanggungan (TK/3)
  • Kawin dgn 1 tanggungan (K/1)
  • Kawin dgn 2 tanggungan (K/2)

Category C

  • Kawin dgn 3 tanggungan (K/3)
